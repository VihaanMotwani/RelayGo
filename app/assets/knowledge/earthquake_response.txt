EARTHQUAKE
DROP, COVER, HOLD ON

INDOORS: Get under sturdy table, hold on. Stay away from windows. Don't run outside.

OUTDOORS: Move to open area away from buildings and power lines. Drop and cover head.

IN CAR: Pull over, avoid bridges/overpasses, stay inside.

NEAR COAST: After shaking stops, go to high ground immediately - tsunami risk.

AFTER: Expect aftershocks. Check for gas leaks. Don't use elevators.
