CPR - ADULT
1. Check if responsive - tap and shout
2. Call for help, get AED
3. Push hard/fast center of chest: 2 inches deep, 100-120/min
4. 30 compressions, then 2 breaths
5. Continue until AED arrives or help takes over

HANDS-ONLY CPR: Just push hard and fast, no breaths needed

AED: Turn on, follow voice prompts, attach pads, shock if advised, resume CPR
