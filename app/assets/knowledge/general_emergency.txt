GENERAL EMERGENCY

CALL 911 FOR: Life threats, fire, serious injury, crime in progress

IF NETWORKS DOWN: Text first (uses less bandwidth), try WiFi calling, use mesh apps

STAY CALM: Panic kills. Breathe. Think. Act.

HELP OTHERS: Speak calmly, listen, provide water/blanket, reassure help is coming

TRIAGE: Treat life-threatening but survivable injuries first
