BLEEDING: Apply direct pressure with cloth. Don't remove - add more layers. Elevate above heart.

SHOCK: Lay flat, elevate legs 12 inches, keep warm, no food/drink.

BURNS: Cool under running water 10-20 min. No ice or butter. Cover loosely.

FRACTURES: Don't move it. Splint in place. Ice wrapped in cloth.

CHOKING: Stand behind, fist above navel, thrust inward and upward. Repeat until clear.
