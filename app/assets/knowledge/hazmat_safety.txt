HAZMAT - GET AWAY
Move: upwind, upstream, uphill, far away

GAS LEAK: Don't use switches/phones/flames. Open windows. Leave. Call from distance.

CHEMICAL ON SKIN: Remove clothes, flush with water 20 min

CHEMICAL IN EYES: Flush with water 20 min, hold lids open

CHEMICAL INHALED: Get to fresh air, start CPR if not breathing

SHELTER IN PLACE: Close all doors/windows, turn off ventilation, seal gaps, go to interior room
