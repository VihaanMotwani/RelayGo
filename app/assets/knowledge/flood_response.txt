FLOOD RESPONSE
1. Move to higher ground immediately
2. Never walk through moving water - 6 inches knocks you down
3. Never drive through floods - 12 inches floats a car
4. "Turn Around Don't Drown"

IF TRAPPED IN BUILDING: Go to highest floor (not attic), signal from window

IF TRAPPED IN CAR: Unbuckle, open/break window, climb to roof

AFTER: Don't touch electrics if wet, boil water, watch for snakes
